This is the documentation of the iba package. Our attribution method
is described in our paper `"Restricting the Flow: Information Bottlenecks for
Attribution" <https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.00396>`_.
Please see our `README <https://github.com/BioroboticsLab/IBA>`_
for installation and short overview.
We also provide a few examplary
`jupyter notebooks <https://github.com/BioroboticsLab/IBA/tree/master/notebooks>`_.
